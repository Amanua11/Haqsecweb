Our Company

Built on Integrity. Driven by Excellence.

Founded in 2014 in Addis Ababa, Haq Security and Cleaning Services was established with a single mission: to provide enterprises and diplomatic missions across Ethiopia with protection and facility management they can genuinely rely on.

What began as a small team of rigorously trained security professionals has grown into a full-service firm serving embassies, banks, corporate headquarters, residential estates, and active construction sites throughout the country.

Our leadership team brings together decades of combined experience in security operations, facility management, and regulatory compliance, ensuring every client receives a service standard that meets and exceeds international benchmarks.

  • All personnel vetted, background-checked, and continuously trained
  • Fully licensed under Ethiopian regulatory authorities
  • Tailored service plans for every sector and scale
  • Rapid response and dedicated account management
